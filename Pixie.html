🐕 Pixie 🧚

An agility - loving ball maniac Jack Russel and Foxterrier mix ⚾

Pixie 🧚 is a rescue pup from the glorious Hungarian city of Tatabanya. She loves to eat, play ⚾, and do agility. She is a mix of 2 terriers (English 🇬🇧 working dogs) so she always has a lot of energy. Terriers are also very loyal and devoted, which is also true for Pixie - she clearly knows her place in the pack and she loves us with all her (huge) heart. Moreover, terriers are also known for their intelligence. This is why Pixie is not only exceling at agility but she also has an obedience diploma granted by the KutyaGuru dog school of Budapest 🇭🇺.
